---
Social isolation has become a crisis and it has left many without a proper emotional outlet. One way to process emotions is through writing, more specifically, diary writing or journaling because it aids the writer in organizing their thoughts and emotions to be written down. However, even though journaling can be helpful, it requires a high cognitive load, whether it is from the blank page syndrome, time commitment, or the difficulty to organize complex emotions into text. As such, individuals will use their energy to vent or verbalize their emotions instead. While venting creates temporary relief, the lack of structure prevents emotional processing, therefore, it is evident that those who need emotional processing are the individuals that lack the bandwidth to do so.

Vently helps relieve users from the cognitive load of diary writing by using an AI chatbot that does the heavy lifting, providing a seamless conversational experience to the user. Once the session comes to an end, the system autonomously transforms the conversation into a structured diary entry that users can access at any time to facilitate self-awareness or to identify emotional patterns. With this system, Vently provides an immediate, judgement-free space for self-reflection, turning emotions into actionable insights.
